Spamty Premium users (premium subscription is not publicly available yet - the iframe is available for every user until the premium subscription is buyable) can use an iframe to display the Captcha and the email address directly on their own website. You don't need to redirect your users with a link to our website.

Live Demo

How it works

First you need to create your Spamty link. You link will look like: 

https://spamty.eu/mail/v4/20/869656557u8375992c/

Open this prettified URL in your browser and it will redirect you to the raw URL - similar to: 

https://spamty.eu/mail.php?version=v4&email=20&key=869656557u8375992c

The last step is to repalce mail.php with mail-frame.php. You can test your URL now. It should show the page which will be displayed as iframe in the next step. 

https://spamty.eu/mail-frame.php?version=v4&email=20&key=869656557u8375992c

Building an iframe

The following code is our iframe. Where you should replace MAIL_FRAME_URL with the URL from above. You can also adjust the message that will be displayed if a browser doesn't support iframes.

<iframe
   src="MAIL_FRAME_URL"
   width="100%" 
   height="300" 
   name="Spamty email address"
   sandbox="allow-scripts allow-popups allow-forms">
   <p>iframes do not work in your browser. You can <a href="MAIL_FRAME_URL" target="_blank" title="Spamty" rel="nofollow">find my email address here</a>.</p>
</iframe>

The sandbox attribute values allow-scripts and allow-popups are required to display a help text when the users clicks on the info link. allow-forms is required to process the captcha form.

Customization

Language

You can change the language of your form by appending &lang=CC to your URL. For example French with: 

https://spamty.eu/mail-frame.php?version=v4&email=20&key=869656557u8375992c&lang=fr

You can use:

  • &lang=ar for Arabic (experimental)
  • &lang=de for German
  • &lang=en for English (default)
  • &lang=es for Spanish (incomplete)
  • &lang=fr for French
  • &lang=zh for Chinese (incomplete)
